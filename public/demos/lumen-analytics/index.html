Questions

Everything you might ask

Do I need a data team to use Lumen?
Not at all. Lumen is built for product, growth, and marketing teams. If you can build a spreadsheet, you can build a Lumen dashboard.
How long does setup take?
Most teams are live in under an hour. Connect a source (Segment, Stripe, or your warehouse), and Lumen models your data automatically.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, support SSO/SAML, role-based access, and offer EU data residency on Enterprise plans.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. There are no contracts on Starter or Growth — cancel from your dashboard with one click.
What happens after my trial ends?
You'll move to the free Starter plan automatically. Nothing is deleted, and you can upgrade again whenever you like.
