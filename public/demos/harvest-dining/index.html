From the kitchen

From the farm, to the fire.

We work with a handful of local growers and cook almost everything over a single wood fire. Nothing is flown in, nothing is fussed over — the ingredient does the talking.

The menu changes every week with what's at its best, so no two visits are ever quite the same.
